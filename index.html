About Tonnerdo

Founded in 2010, Tonnerdo has been providing exceptional dry cleaning services to the community. Our team of experienced professionals uses state-of-the-art equipment and eco-friendly cleaning solutions to ensure your garments receive the best care possible.

We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, excellent customer service, and commitment to quality. Whether it's a special occasion dress, a business suit, or everyday clothing, we treat every item with the care it deserves.

Our mission is to deliver superior cleaning services with integrity, professionalism, and a personal touch. We believe that clean clothes should be easy to achieve, which is why we offer convenient pickup and delivery options.
